Coming Out of Nowhere

Est. Summer 2023

Game Design: Bradley Estacio, Illustration: Yasaman Mostafavi and Kelly Tan

"Anywhere but Here, anywhere but Nowhere."

Coming Out of Nowhere is a 2-6 player game where a group of seemingly misfit teens band together to escape a dead-end town as they discovered that the local sheriff has been brainwashing people. Work together and tag along with your newfound friends as you collect items and plan out your best means of escape. Make sure to avoid the sheriff and his deputy partner patrolling this idle town. Time's ticking before you're brainwashed for good.

Player Count: 2-6, Playtime: 30 mins., Ages: 12+

All of Your Beeswax!

Coming Soon...

Game Design and Lead Development: Joshua Prila, Artist: Kelly Tan

All of Your Beeswax is a game about managing a beehive. Rain or shine, they'll keep making honey! Plan out your hive by exploring different ways to build up your resources. How will you prioritize to keep up with mother nature? Scale and grow your hive to maintain your busy bees.

Genre: Single player city-builder

Twilight: Of Courts and Contracts

Coming Soon...

Game Design: Bradley Estacio, Illustrator: Kelly Tan

In the Realm of Faerie, the Fey Courts can agree on two things: rules are malleable, and contracts are binding. Play as the ever-so-loved and benevolent Faerie Queene. Take root as the Lord of the Forest and slowly encroach on the other Courts. Plot and scheme as the Trickster Envoy and undermine the competition. Or rise up with ferocious power as the Imperator Animalis and prove your might. Which court can come up on top to establish themselves as the great Fey Court for the next century?

Player Count: 2-4, Playtime: 90-120 mins., Ages: 14+
